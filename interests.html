Bouldering

Other than wokring out at the gym, bouldering is the physical activity of choice that I picked up less than 6 months ago, yet I am already in love with it and look forward to tackling different problems every time. Both physical conditioning and mental fortitude are required to perservere through the hardest of problems and I find myself trying over and over again until I have the last laugh by reaching the highest hold. I highly recommend trying bouldering by yourself or with friends!

Baking

Baking is a very relaxing pastime that I picked up when I was 16. It requires a decent amount of precision which excites the ex-chemistry student in me and the various foods I can try really satisfies the inner foodie (glutton) in me. The first bite of a successful end-product always feels the greatest but even subpar end-products can help me make adjustments to the recipe and baking procedure so that it can become more refined each time I make it.

Travelling

I have to travelled to various countries in my life and it is always super refreshing to see the differences in culture, cuisine, architecture, and natural scenery of every region. There are so many moments from my trips that I wish to relive, but flights are expensive so I will have to relive my memories through some photos for the moment.

Anime

My favourite medium of story telling is anime, and watching anime is one of myfavourite activities for spare time and for breaks in between studying. The story telling-methodsand animation quality of animation studios have evolved so much over the past decade and really elevates the emotion, suspense, power of the original stories. Some of my favourite and recommended animes are Demon Slayer, 86, Chainsaw Man, Steins;Gate, and Mob Psycho 100.
